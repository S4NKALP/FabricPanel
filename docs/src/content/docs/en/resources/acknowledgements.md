## Inspirations

Tsumiki was shaped by projects that pushed modular bars in the Wayland ecosystem:

- **Waybar**: major inspiration for panel concepts and workflow.
  [Waybar on GitHub](https://github.com/Alexays/Waybar)
- **HyprPanel**: inspiration for dynamic Hyprland-focused panel behavior.
  [HyprPanel on GitHub](https://github.com/Jas-SinghFSU/HyprPanel)
